Lobster Bisque

a lobster based soup

Ingredients

  • lobster
  • onions
  • garlic
  • carrots
  • celery
  • potatoes
  • flour
  • butter
  • milk
  • cream
  • pepper
  • salt

Steps

  1. Chop vegetables
  2. Boil water and cook lobster
  3. Heat pan and add butter
  4. Add onions and garlic and cook for 2 minutes
  5. Add carrots and celery and cook for 2 minutes
  6. Add potatoes and cook for 2 minutes
  7. Add flour and cook for 2 minutes
  8. Add milk and cook for 2 minutes
  9. Add cream and cook for 2 minutes
  10. Add lobster and cook for 2 minutes
  11. Add salt and pepper to taste
