Curry Goat

a dish that is popular in many countries around the world, including the Caribbean, Africa, and the United Kingdom. It is made with goat meat that has been cooked in a spicy curry sauce.

Ingredients

  • Cumin
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Rice
  • Goat meat
  • Curry powder
  • Coconut Milk

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook rice
  2. Chop onions and garlic
  3. Heat pan and add oil
  4. Add onions and garlic and cook for 2 minutes
  5. Add goat meat and cook for 5 minutes
  6. Add curry powder and cook for 2 minutes
  7. Add coconut milk and cook for 2 minutes
  8. Add salt and pepper to taste
