Yakisoba

A chicken and noodle based dish complimented with vegetables and sauce

Ingredients

  • Grilled chicken
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Green peppers
  • Broccoli
  • Yakisoba noodles
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook noodles
  2. Chop vegetables
  3. Grill chicken
  4. Heat pan and add oil
  5. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute
  6. Add vegetables and cook for 5 minutes
  7. Add chicken and cook for 2 minutes
  8. Add noodles and cook for 2 minutes
  9. Add soy sauce and cook for 1 minute
  10. Add salt and pepper to taste
